A creative design studio located in New Milton supplying made to order furniture for the residential interiors industry.

Made to order service, We design, We order, We fit

Studio 30 Interiors are a specialised design led company that produce distinctive interiors to make your house a home or have your kitchen dreams come true with our expertise in hand.

Our journey started 15 years ago when we first laid eyes on the interior industry starting as an apprentice and then progressing within interior remodelling, focussing in modern, contemporary, and classic which has only helped to develop Studio 30 further over the years.
